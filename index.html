Steven Jerjian

Postdoctoral Research Fellow at Johns Hopkins University, in the Fetsch Lab in the Zanvyl Krieger Mind/Brain Institute.

I obtained my PhD in Movement Neuroscience in December 2019 from the Queen Square Institute of Neurology at University College London, where I was advised by Alexander Kraskov and Maneesh Sahani, and funded by Brain Research UK. I completed my MSci. Natural Sciences degree at UCL in 2015, majoring in Neuroscience.
